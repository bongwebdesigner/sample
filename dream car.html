ROLLS-ROYCE

Today, we announce the highest annual sales results in our 117-year history.
We delivered 5,586 motor cars to clients around the world, an increase of 49% on last year’s total.
We saw extremely strong demand for all Rolls-Royce models, led by #Phantom, our pinnacle product.
Growth was driven principally by our new #Ghost in its first full year of availability, boosted further by the launch of the new #BlackBadge Ghost in October.
This, together with continuing strong demand for our super-luxury SUV, #Cullinan, has filled our order books well into the third quarter of 2022.
Our Provenance (pre-owned) programme also enjoyed exceptional sales results in 2021, achieving an all-time record.
